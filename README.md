This Power BI dashboard provides an end-to-end analysis of shop sales performance across states, cities, product categories, customers, and payment modes. It helps understand revenue trends, profit contribution, customer behavior, and product performance through interactive visuals and dynamic filters.

Key Features:-
1. State-Wise & City-Wise Filters
Allows users to slice the entire report by state and city.
All visuals update instantly based on the selected region.

2. Revenue & Profit Overview
Total Revenue Generated: 114K
Total Profit Earned: 11K
KPI cards give a quick financial snapshot of overall business performance.

3. State-Wise Revenue Breakdown
Horizontal bar chart showing revenue contribution from:
Maharashtra
Uttar Pradesh
Madhya Pradesh
Himachal Pradesh
Delhi
Helps identify the strongest and weakest markets.

4. Category-Wise Revenue
Visualizes revenue generated by major product categories:
Electronics
Clothing
Furniture
Useful for understanding which category performs best.

5. Top Customers
Identifies key customers driving revenue:
Harivansh (highest)
Lalita
Gopal
Priyanka
Helps with targeted marketing and customer retention strategies.

6. Best Performing Products
Line chart showcasing top-selling products:
Saree
Bookcases
Printers
Stole
Trousers
Tracks the sales trend of each product.

7. Quarter-Wise Filter
Users can filter data by Q1, Q2, Q3, or Q4.
Enables seasonal trend analysis.

8. Payment Mode Analysis
Donut chart showing revenue share through:
COD, Credit Card, UPI, EMI, Debit Card
Helps in understanding customer payment preferences.

Summary:-
This dashboard serves as a comprehensive analytical tool for understanding overall shop performance. It highlights:
Revenue & profit metrics
Market performance by state/city
Customer purchase behavior
Product category insights
Seasonal and payment mode trends
Ideal for business owners, analysts, and decision-makers who want a complete view of sales performance in one place.
